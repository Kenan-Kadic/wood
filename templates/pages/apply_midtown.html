Since 1990, Midtown Home Improvements has been working hard for our customers to ensure their satisfaction, and we know that all starts with our employees. As your local roofer and general contractor, we rely on our staff members, and we treat them like family. In fact, all our employees are committed and held accountable to the following family values:

  • Growth-oriented mindset
  • Positive character
  • Dedication to their job
  • Initiative
  • Trust

In return, Midtown Home Improvements ensures that our workplace is welcoming and friendly. Don’t just take our word for it. We have been named by the St. Louis Post-Dispatch as one of the top places to work. We offer paid employee health insurance, 401(k) matching and vacation packages. We are also dedicated to adequately training our employees, which is why we offer multiple paid training programs. We currently employ over 40 full-time and over 30 part-time employees.
