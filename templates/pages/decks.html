PREMIUM UNDER DECKING SYSTEMS

Another premium product from Midtown is InsideOut® Underdecking Rain Carrying Systems. InsideOut is the best and easiest way to create a great outdoor space under your raised deck. It is made of vinyl ceiling panels that collect and drain water away. It provides a maintenance-free ceiling that covers the unsightly underside of your deck, turning it into a clean, bright, dry outdoor space. You can change your space into a screened porch, add a hot tub or just use it as a comfortable outdoor room to relax. You can even use the InsideOut system to create dry storage for lawn equipment, bikes and more. Benefits of InsideOut Underdecking include

  • All-weather protection.
  • 10-year limited warranty.
